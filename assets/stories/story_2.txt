In a stormy country, a curious penguin met a monster who loved to learn. Another day, the penguin asked, "Do you know your diet?" The monster said, "I eat fish and berries!" They touched flippers and started a sequence of fun facts. The penguin showed off his glossary of animal sounds. But then, a breezy wind blew, making it hard to finish. Just as they were about to give up, an electric current sparked, lighting up pictograms that revealed a secret fact: "Gurgle" was a sound made by fish in the ocean!
