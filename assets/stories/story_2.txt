Once upon a time in a magical land far, far away, there was a little star named Twinkle who loved to twinkle high up in the sky. One day, Preetiest the fairy flew down from her home in a beautiful meadow and said "Twinkle, your sparkle is the wonder of all our land!" Together they went on an adventure to find the most enchanted forest, where magic happened every night under the starry sky. As they walked, Twinkle's light shone brighter, making the flowers bloom and the animals dance with joy. The fairy and Twinkle became the best of friends, spreading wonder and magic wherever they went. From that day on, whenever you gaze up at the stars, remember to spread a little bit of your own sparkle and wonder.

Moral: A small spark of kindness can make a big difference in someone's life!
