In the land of Animalville, a penguin named Percy loved to plan adventures. He compare his best friends' colours and choose the most breezy day for their outing. On the stormy day, they visited the enormous pyramid made by ants. Labels on the walls told stories of brave creatures who never gave up. A horrid storm came, but Percy's friends had grit and helped him find shelter. Tiptoeing through the wet grass, they stalked a fish to share for lunch. As they ate, Percy asked them to describe their favourite memory with him. Remember, sharing is caring!
