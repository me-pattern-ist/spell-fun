One sunny day, animals gathered about a shiny coin on the ground. A little bird asked, "How it got here?" A wise old owl said, "It fell from a sky around the square where we live." The birds started to make sentences and describe their homes. They watched as a fox began to stalk its prey, but didn't want to scare anyone. Next to the coin stood a pyramid, reminding them of movement in their community. A cockerel let out a loud call while the others knew it was time for fun. When it's back to nature, we need nothing more.
