One sunny day in the park, Percy the penguin showed everyone how to flip a fish! "Gobble, gobble!" he said as he dove into the water. Those who watched knew he was a pro! But when a stormy cloud rolled in, Percy had an idea. He made a gloopy poster that said, "Don't scare people with storms!" It showed how to explain what's happening and make everyone feel safe. After a nap, Percy woke up and said, "The secret ingredients for bravery are grit and quick thinking." And from then on, he was the hero of the park!
