In the land of Fantasy, there lived a Gloopy monster named Grumble. He loved to gobble up all sorts of tasty treats like juicy berries and sweet honey. One day, while Grumble was out for a stroll, he met a friendly wizard who said, "You know what's even better than eating gloopy foods? Making them!" The wizard showed Grumble how to compare his favorite recipe with others, and soon they were cooking up a squelch-filled storm together. Together, they created the most scrumptious dish in all of Fantasy, and everyone celebrated with a big gobble-filled feast!
