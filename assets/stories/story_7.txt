Theme: Fantasy
Words: Sequence, Stormy, Sentences, Ingredients, Electic, Pretend, Wonder, Ocean, Nap, Want, Flip, Mask, Daintily, Back, Square, Country, Write, Tough, Caption, About

In a magical kingdom, a brave knight wanted to flip through the pages of an old book. The sentences told a sequence of adventures beneath a stormy ocean. She gathered ingredients for a special potion and added a pinch of magic sparkles. Suddenly, an electric current hummed, making her pretend she was a fish in the sea! As she swam, she wondered what lay back on dry land. She flipped a mask over her head daintily and started to write about her journey. Back home, she wanted to tell everyone about it - but first, she took a nap under a square flag in the country.
