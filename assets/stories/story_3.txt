Once upon a time, on a beautiful shore, there lived a little bird named Luna. She loved to fly over the ocean, watching the seagulls soar high with their magnificent plumes. One day, while exploring the tide pools, Luna found a special stalk of seaweed that grew just for her. As she munched on the snack, she heard the noisy chirping of her friends from afar. The birds gathered together to create a colorful mural by the shore, illustrating all the amazing things they had discovered that day. Luna felt grateful for her magical adventure and the wonderful friends she shared it with.

Always appreciate nature's little wonders that surround you.
