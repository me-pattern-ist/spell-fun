One sunny day in the forest, a curious rabbit named Rosie wanted to find the pretiest flower. She heard a croak and followed the sound. A big frog named Freddy said, "You'll know it's me when you see my enormous voice!" They started chasing each other until they found the most beautiful flowers. To make them easier to spot, Freddy added special features like sparkly petals. But then, someone came by and bent the flowers! Rosie knew just what to do – she squelched softly on tiptoe and touched the muddy part. It was a sneaky solution!
