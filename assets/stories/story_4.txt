In a land far, far away, a very silly monster lived in a swampy cave. He loved to measure the tea with a teaspoon and make yummy brews. One day, he made an awful mess! But then he chose to join the garden fair, where colorful plumes grew on the plants. A gentle guide showed him how to plant seeds and make patterns on the ground. The monster's diet changed from swampy snacks to juicy fruits and veggies. He even learned a new verse for his mask: "Gurgle, gurgle, I'm so happy!" Moral: Kindness can help clean up any mess!
