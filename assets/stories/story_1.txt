In a sunny meadow, a group of friends went on an adventure without a map. Your leader, Max, told them to follow their instincts and check for clues along the way. A gusty wind blew, but they didn't tempt fate – they kept moving forward. Suddenly, lightning flashed, and a loud clap of thunder made everyone jump! The kids wondered what was about to happen next. As they crested a hill, they saw a bunch of sparkling waterfalls. It was an ending they never expected! A wise guide appeared and said, "Swirl around the cave entrance, and you'll find your way."
